## 9. Barakode Interactive Cost Calculator
The Barakode Cost Calculator is an advanced, automated tool on the website that helps clients generate instant, highly accurate project estimates based on their specific needs. It serves two distinct calculation models:

1. **Project Build Mode**: Ideal for clients looking to build a custom software project from scratch. It allows users to:
   - Select platforms (Web, iOS, Android, etc.).
   - Define project basics (UI/UX design needs, backend development, etc.).
   - Pick granular features (Authentication, Payment Gateways, AI integration, etc.).
   - Leverage an integrated **AI Analyzer** that takes a plain-text prompt of the project idea and instantly recommends the necessary technical features and estimates the cost.
   - Compare different platform or feature combinations side-by-side.

2. **Team Scaling Mode**: Designed for businesses looking to augment their existing workforce under the Dedicated Talent Hiring Model. It features:
   - A **Team Architect** where clients can adjust the number of full-stack developers, AI engineers, designers, etc., that they wish to hire and see the immediate monthly/yearly cost.
   - An **ROI Dashboard** that calculates potential savings compared to hiring an in-house team in markets like the US or Europe.

The calculator provides a continuous **Live Estimate** updating in real-time as users adjust parameters. It makes Barakode's pricing completely transparent, saving hours of back-and-forth discovery calls.
